Our Story

Founded in 2021, Sri Radha Krishna PG began with a simple mission: to provide comfortable, safe and affordable accommodation for working professionals and students in Hinjawadi, Pune's thriving IT hub.

What started as a single property in Mukai Nagar has now grown to two premium PG accommodations serving hundreds of residents with our signature blend of homely comfort and modern amenities.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for reliability, food quality, cleanliness and excellent service, becoming one of the most trusted names in PG accommodation in Hinjawadi.

Our Mission

To provide a home away from home for young professionals and students through comfortable living spaces, nutritious food and a supportive community.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted PG accommodation provider in Pune by consistently delivering exceptional living experiences at affordable prices.

Our Values

Cleanliness, Safety, Comfort, Community and Continuous Improvement are the core values that guide everything we do.
